About Murree

Murree is one of the most famous hill stations in Pakistan. Its cool climate, pine-covered mountains, and serene beauty attract thousands of visitors each year. It's an ideal place to relax, explore nature, and enjoy the mountain breeze.

Top Attractions

  • Mall Road: Shopping and delicious local food.
  • Pindi Point: Panoramic mountain views.
  • Kashmir Point: Calm and peaceful surroundings.
  • Patriata (New Murree): Chair lifts & cable car rides.
  • Ayubia National Park: Forest walks & picnic spots.
